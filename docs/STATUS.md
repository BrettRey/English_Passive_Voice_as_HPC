## Working Thesis Direction

The paper should not argue merely that the English passive has fuzzy boundaries. It should argue that English passive voice is best treated as a maintained, projectible construction family or cluster, with overlapping formal and discourse cues rather than a single short checklist.

Pullum is descriptive groundwork, not the whole argument. The book-level contribution is the stronger claim that the passive should be analyzed through maintenance and projectibility rather than definitional essence.

## Constraints

3. The empirical section should stay bounded enough that the project remains a theory paper rather than turning into a large annotation-methods paper.
